Connect 4

It's the classic game you love to play with your friends and family.
Can you connect four of your coloured disks by dropping them into the holder before your opponent does?

Try it now!

Rules

  • Point the cursor over the row you wish to drop your piece in

  • Left click to drop your piece

  • When you can connect four pieces vertically, horizontally or diagonally you win
